Villas & Wellness Resort

SIWA Cliffs consist of a Clifftop Club, Restaurant, Fitness & Wellness center, and collection of luxury 2 or 3 bedroom pool villa properties located high above the seaside town of Kuta, South Lombok. These sustainably constructed luxury ocean view and Rinjani view properties are the first part of this boutique wellness destination welcoming guests in 2022.

Perfectly located

SIWA is the perfect location, offering convenience, privacy and inspiring ocean views. The town of Kuta has established itself as a hub for the surrounding area offering visitors excellent dining, shopping and entertainment choice.
